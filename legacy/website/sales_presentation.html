Reference

Glossary

NCA ECC:National Cybersecurity Authority Essential Cybersecurity Controls - Saudi mandatory security framework.
SDAIA PDPL:Saudi Data & AI Authority Personal Data Protection Law - Privacy regulation similar to GDPR.
SAMA:Saudi Arabian Monetary Authority - Central bank regulating payment services.
Multi-Tenancy:Architecture where one software instance serves multiple customers with isolated data.
Edge Infrastructure:Distributed computing nodes closer to users for faster response times.
SSR:Server-Side Rendering - Pages generated on server for faster initial load and SEO.
