# User Manual (Doctors & Nurses)

## Overview

This manual describes how Doctors and Nurses use ClinicFlow to manage patient care and clinical workflows.

## Login process

1. Open the ClinicFlow portal.
2. Enter your clinic `Account ID`.
3. Enter your username and password.
4. If successful, you are authenticated for your clinic and role.

## Doctor workflow

### Access patient records

- Search or browse patients associated with your clinic.
- View patient demographic details, contact information, and medical history.
- Access visit notes, prescriptions, and appointment summaries.

### Manage consultations

- Create new patient encounters or visit records.
- Add diagnostic notes and treatment recommendations.
- Document follow-up instructions and next appointment details.

### Review appointments

- View the clinic appointment schedule for the day.
- Confirm patient arrival and clinical status.
- Track patient flow by appointment slot.

### Clinical notes and handoff

- Use the patient record to capture important clinical details.
- Share information with Nurses using in-app notes or handoff fields.
- Ensure that care instructions are clear and complete.

## Nurse workflow

### Patient intake

- Register patients under the correct clinic tenant.
- Verify identity details and patient contact information.
- Collect relevant insurance or payment details if required.

### Visit preparation

- Review the upcoming schedule for assigned clinicians.
- Prepare patient records and documentation for the next appointment.
- Record vital signs and preparatory details if supported.

### In-clinic support

- Update the patient record after triage or initial assessment.
- Add notes for the Doctor to review before consultation.
- Mark appointments as completed or rescheduled.

## General user guidelines

- Always verify the correct clinic `Account ID` before logging in.
- Do not share passwords; maintain account security.
- Confirm that patient records are updated immediately after each visit.
- Respect data privacy and only access patients for your assigned clinic.

## Best practices

- Use strong, unique passwords.
- Log out of the system when you finish your shift.
- Report any suspicious activity or data access issues to administration.
- Collaborate with administrative staff for patient scheduling and billing questions.

## Future enhancements for user experience

- Add a patient search and filter panel.
- Support direct appointment creation and rescheduling.
- Offer clinical dashboards for daily case load and task lists.
- Provide role-specific in-app help content for Doctors and Nurses.
